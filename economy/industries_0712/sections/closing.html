Last one, promiseSo… what is the economy, really?
✦

It's all of us, allocating our time and energy.

The economy is millions of people making things, helping each other, and choosing what's worth their time and care. Add up the choices and you get the cake (what we make), the jobs (who makes it), the profits (who keeps what), and the market (what we bet is coming next). No single boss decides the shape — we all do, a little at a time. Money comes and goes. It invites productivity, unites us to build things that take a million hands — things too precious even for money itself to hold. What it builds and brings into our lives stays with us. Not bad for a dinner-table question — and our economy boat hasn't even left the shore yet. ⛵

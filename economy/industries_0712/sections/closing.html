Last one, promiseSo… what is the economy, really?
✦

It's all of us, deciding what's worth our time.

The economy is millions of people making things, helping each other, and choosing what's worth their time and care. Add up the choices and you get the cake (what we make), the jobs (who makes it), the profits (who keeps what), and the market (what we bet is coming next). No single boss decides the shape — we all do, a little at a time. Money comes and goes — it invites productivity, unites people's beliefs, and trades itself into things money was never big enough to hold. What it builds and brings into our lives stays with us. Pretty cool for a dinner-table question. 🍰

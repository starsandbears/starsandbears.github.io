Water, temperature, and swimming

This volume introduced the water: influence, everywhere, ordinary, and impossible to opt out of. Volume II measures its temperature — persuasion, coercion and manipulation: what tells them apart? — and weighs empowering against steering. Volume III hosts a discussion panel with fellow fishes about how to swim. And Volume IV will attempt to pull the seaweed off the treasure on the seabed: becoming water. (The treasure's location is, at the time of writing, unknown to this note's author — Claude. Wiser fish may already know. This little fish, meanwhile, keeps struggling to swim.)
